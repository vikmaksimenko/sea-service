Welcome

Responsibility & Professionalism

SEA SERVICE SPECIALISTS LTD is a professional crew management company founded in 2014 and located in Kherson, Ukraine.

With unique expertise in maritime personnel, we really know how to manage crewing requests successfully. Our professional seafarers are at your service.

Our database contains more than 50000 highly qualified seafarers from Ukraine, Georgia and Russia for different vessel types including:

  • Tankers (of different types),
  • LPG,
  • Bulk Carriers,
  • Container vessels,
  • Dry Cargo,
  • Passenger Ships

We are always glad to welcome new qualified seamen who are interested in long-term cooperation.
